Technical Writing Projects

Nutanix CE Installation Guide (Spec Work)

A local MSP needed an installation guide for Nutanix Community Edition for its engineers to reduce configuration errors and improve process uniformity across installation engagements at client sites.

Leveraging available vendor documentation and personal experience, I created an install guide easily accessible to company field techs who had little experience with the Nutanix platform. The document is broken into sections by topic, and I use visual indicators throughout to indicate where extra caution is needed, or when a step could have a severe impact on the platform if performed incorrectly.

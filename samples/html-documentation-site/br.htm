The <br> Element

The <br> element allows you to insert a line break in your text. It should not be used to separate paragraphs.

Example:

                    
                        <p class="example-code">
                            Cat ipsum dolor sit amet, chew foot yet kitty power but playing with balls of wool. <br />
                            Bite the neighbor's bratty kid.<br />
                            Hunt by meowing loudly at 5am next to human slave food dispenser.<br />
                        </p>

Result:

Cat ipsum dolor sit amet, chew foot yet kitty power but playing with balls of wool.
Bite the neighbor's bratty kid.
Hunt by meowing loudly at 5am next to human slave food dispenser.

For more details, please see the wiki page for this element at w3c.org.
